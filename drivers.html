Driver Options

Over the road
If you want to hit the open road and explore the country, over-the-road truck driving is the way to go. You'll average 600 miles per haul, enjoying the freedom that comes with a great truck driving career. This is one of the best ways to become a million-mile driver.
Team
You can expect to see between 5,000 and 6,000 miles per week. Racking up miles like that means you'll be bringing home bigger paychecks. In fact, teams are among the highest compensated drivers in the industry, with many earning over $70,000 a year.
Regional
Serve customers in a five-state area of where you live and enjoy the advantages of working with local dispatchers. They’re located right in your area — meaning they’re familiar with the weather conditions and the traffic patterns. Regional truck driving is a great option for a driver looking for stable, predictable home time. After all, driving close to home makes it easier to get there more often.
Dedicated Route
Clockwork's dedicated route drivers get home weekly or more — typically on the same days each week. Pulling freight for one Dedicated customer means you can develop a routine. You’ll leave home about the same time each week, travel on familiar lanes at predictable times, then return home on schedule. This setup allows you to make plans with your family and friends. They’ll be happy to know they can count on you to be home when you say you’ll be there.
